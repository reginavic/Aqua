Membership

The swimming pools are complimented by relaxing hot spa pools to soothe away the aches and stresses of the day, along with truly luxurious beautiful fibre optic lit steam rooms to complete the relaxation program - you won't want to go home!
You can either become a member or pay for one-off swim sessions.
Children aged under seven years are able to swim for free.
Swim safety rules apply.

Personal Training

You don't have to be a pro to use the swimming pool. All of our pools offer expert swim tuition. Training run for 40–60 minutes and are available for all abilities – from total beginners to those just wanting to improve their technique.

Group Training

A fantastic cardiovascular sport, swimming works not only your heart to improve stamina, but many other muscles in the body such as the arms, legs and core.
Suitable for all ages and fitness abilities.
Swimming is a low-impact way to burn calories and tone up.
Our group programs include Aqua Basic, Aqua Sphere, Aqua ABS, Aqua Motion, Warm-Up, Aqua Jogger and much more!
